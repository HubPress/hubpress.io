<p><strong>Recipe 1:</strong></p>
</div>
<div class="ulist">
<ul>
<li>
<p>1000 g flour (wheat or spelt)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>60% - 65% water (60% with spelt)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>2% salt</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>yeast</p>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div class="paragraph">
<p>Knead, proof over night, pre-heat oven (incl. baking stone) and a cast iron pot with lid (Dutch oven) to 265 C or the maximum temperature of you oven.</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph">
<p>Form the loaf and proof (on a sheet of baking parchment) until well risen.</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph">
<p>Move loaf with the parchment into the pre-heated pot, return to the oven, turn down the oven to 250 C and bake for 1 hour.</p>
</div>
