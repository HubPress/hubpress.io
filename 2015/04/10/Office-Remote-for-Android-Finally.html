Office Remote for Android - Finally

10 April 2015 on Android, Apps

Last week on Product Hunt an app called Swoosh made it’s way that interested me - a way to control PowerPoint presentations by using a gesture over your Android phone. Really cool. All you needed was a server on your computer (Windows, Mac, and Linux friendly), the app (which cost $1.99), and you’re good to go (Google Play). I did not purchase it because I thought "$1.99? Come on." Well it sounds like someone heard my cry at Microsoft as they have released (or updated?) yesterday called Offive Remote for Android (Google Play). A bluetooth enabled preentation, Microsoft Word, and Excel geture (finger gesture) swipping on an Android phone so you don’t have to keep operating at the computer. A helpful little app to make your presentations easier and run smoothly!
